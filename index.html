LeetRecur

Master Leetcode with Spaced Repetition

Easy Management

LeetRecur tracks your leetcode problem submissions and manages all the relevant statistics for you, making it completely hands-free.

Now you can focus on problem solving instead of managing your submissions and pondering which problem require more of your effort.

AI Powered

It accurately predicts your proficiency for each problem based on your activity while solving that problem and automatically schedule its revision with Spaced Repetition - a proven evidence-based learning technique.

Gone are the days when you had to worry about forgetting hard learned concepts.

Minimal

It includes a minimal popup which shows you what questions you should retry.

It also includes a home page where you can view your stats for all the problems in order of your proficiency for each.

Privacy policy

I care about your privacy, just like you do. Please find below how this extension operates and what data it collects.

Introducing the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) explains how LeetRecur processes information. LeetRecur products include, but are not limited to, the LeetRecur extension for web browsers (the “Extension”) and the website located at leetrecur.namanshrimali.com (the “Website”).

I do not collect your data. All the data and analytics that the extension generates is stored in your local machine, and is cleared as soon as you remove the extension. The extension only works for problems listed under https://leetcode.com/problems and does not operate over other websites.

Changes to this policy

I will continue to update our policies and practices as needed. I will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting any changes here. If I do, you’ll see that the date at the top of this Privacy Policy has changed.
