LeetRecur

Spaced Repetition for Leetcode

Spaced Repetition

LeetRecur helps you to revise leetcode problems in with spaced reptition - a proven an evidence-based learning technique.

Hands Free

Completely hands-free, it works in the background so that you can focus on problem solving instead of managing your revision strategy.

Minimal

It includes a minimal popup which shows you what questions you should retry, based on spaced repetition algorithm.

It also includes a home page where you can view your stats on your submission in the order of queue.

Privacy policy

We care about your privacy, just like you do. Please find below how this extension operates and what data it collects.

Introducing the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) explains how LeetRecur processes information. LeetRecur products include, but are not limited to, the LeetRecur extension for web browsers (the “Extension”) and the website located at leetrecur.namanshrimali.com (the “Website”).

We do not collect your data. All the data and analytics that the extension generates is stored in your local machine, and is cleared as soon as you remove the extension. The extension only works for problems listed under https://leetcode.com/problems and does not operate over other websites.

Changes to this policy

We will continue to update our policies and practices as needed. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting any changes here. If we do, you’ll see that the date at the top of this Privacy Policy has changed.
